Privacy & how your info is used ✕

What we collect: your first name, your quiz answers, an optional gender and match preference, an optional email/phone/Instagram handle, and — once you reach the booth — your photos.

How it's used: your quiz answers are compared with other guests' to generate a match. Your photos and contact info are printed on your ticket and your match's ticket so you can reach each other.

Who can see it: only staff running the event and, on the printed ticket, the specific guest you're matched with. We don't sell your information.

Social media: your photo is only posted publicly if you checked the separate opt-in box for it. You can decline that and still play.

How long we keep it: event data is deleted after the event ends. If you'd like it deleted sooner, tell a staff member at the booth.

Age: this experience is intended for guests 18 and older.
